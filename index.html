EDUCATION IS IMPORTANT

Education is a key and the power in our lives. It helps you develop critical skills like decision-making, mental agility, problem-solving, and logical thinking. These skills can be applied in our future jobs and in our lives, like when the people face problems in certain situations. We need to go to school, be well, and study hard for the success in future. With education, you can read, write, speak, think critically, make decisions, know right and wrong, effectively communicate, and understand how these things in the world works. While having lack of education, particularly in poor or developing countries, increases the illiterary rate and the number of people of not able to acquire these skills learned in school.

Students who do well in school are better able to make the transition into adulthood and to achieve occupational and economic success.

COMPUTERS ALL AROUND US

We're so fortunate that we have access to computers and other technologies, unlike some countries, particularly in Africa where they have no access to technologies. The simple technology, like the personal computer consists of CPU, monitor, mouse, and keyboard that runs together on a three-step cycle, namely input, process, and output.

Computers give advantages to us, like high speed, conveniency, automation, accuracy, storage, and some ease of access. Now, we can do work much easier with the help of the new technologies, like in medicine, scientific research, and political defense. We can even also give us entertainment like playing video games, reading e-books, and watching movies and videos.

Computers also have their disadvantages too. They can be slow, unreliable, expensive, and the requirement of constant maintenance and upgrades. Hackers could also steal your private data and leak them on the internet, so anyone can access this data. There are also threats like viruses, spams, bug and many other problems. It also gives us some health problems like too much sitting, the posture, back and neck pains, eye strain, and addiction.

So, we should use the computer wisely, do what to do and what not to do when using, manage the time well in using these type of technologies.
